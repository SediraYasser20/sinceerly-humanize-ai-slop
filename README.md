# ✉️ Sinceerly — Free & Open Source

> We now use AI to write emails, and AI to hide that AI wrote them.  
> This is the free, open-source version of that second step.

A Chrome extension that humanizes AI-written emails — removes em dashes, strips robotic phrasing, and adds natural imperfections. Inspired by [Sinceerly](https://sinceerly.com/). Completely free. Bring your own Anthropic API key.

---

## Why

AI emails all look the same.

Perfect grammar. Em dashes everywhere. "I hope this finds you well." "I wanted to reach out regarding potential synergies…"

People feel it before they can explain it. Response rates drop. Connections feel hollow.

The fix is simple: introduce calculated imperfections. A contraction here. A typo near the top. Cut the corporate fluff. Sound like a human who typed fast and hit send.

This extension does exactly that — for free, with no middleman.

---

## Features

- **3 humanization modes** — Subtle, Human, CEO
- **Works inside Gmail** — panel appears directly in your compose window
- **Yellow highlights** — see every change before you approve
- **Bring Your Own API Key (BYOK)** — your key, your data, zero storage
- **No subscription. No cap. No account.**
- **Open source** — fork it, break it, improve it
